Our Mission

Our mission is to provide information regarding the causes of food security and other a few ways someone can help out. Food Insecurity is regarded as the state of being without reliable access to a sufficient quantity of affordable, nutritious food. leading Global causes of Food Security around the globe include poverty, lack of affordable housing, and chronic health conditions

Causes Of Food Insecurity

These are the primary causes of Food Insecurity

Poverty

Poverty is the leading cause of food Insecurity globally. A lack of access to clean water and food, good education, and working infrastructure create breeding grounds of food insecurity as access to nourishment is almost always correlated with a good living situation

Affordable Housing

Lack of Affordable Housing is greatly responsible for high prevalences of food insecurity especially in urban areas. Without affordable housing, many families will have to spend inordinate amounts of money on rent and bill payments neglecting actions like grocery shopping which directly affect the availability of food in that household.

Environmental causes

While still significant, albeit on a lesser note, Environmental causes are also responsible for a great deal of food scarcity globally, especially in areas like south asia where monsoons wreak havoc on the soil and the livelihoods of small-scale farmers. The best way to counteract this would be better education about natural disasters like monsoons and how to avoid + recover from such things.

Fixing Food Insecurity

These are the primary fixes of Food Insecurity

Waste Management

The reduction of food waste is critical on the micro-level in reducing food insecurity, as effecient household food management directly corresponds with a larger availability of food.

Resolving Displacement

When people get displaced, food insecurity tends to comes with them, without effecient refugee camps for migrants from war-torn countries, there becomes a large food gap. The best way to resolve hunger caused by refugee crisis' are to establish social programs that helps refugees re-adjust and provide for themselves outside of government asistence.

Curbing Climate Change

In order to increase global food production, Climate Change must be reduced because of the disastrous effect that global warming has had on global agriculture. This reductio can be acheived by reducing carbon emissions and instead opting for cleaner energy options.
