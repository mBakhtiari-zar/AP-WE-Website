How to Help Out

Below are the notable charities and nonprofits that are responsible for the majority of aid towards food-insecure homes. Donating to any of these reputable organizations will ensure your aid getting into the right hands.

World Food Programme

The WFP is the largest humanitarian organization specializing in food relief around the world. Their aid reaches almost 38 million people per year in 120 countries which makes donating to them a more than reasonable route in taking action against hunger

Feeding America Project

Looking domestically, Feeding America is your best bet as they specialize entirely in providing domestic food support to less fortunate families.

The Hunger Project

The Hunger Project is another large-scale nonprofit organization responsible for providing food to nations throughout South Asia, Africa, and Latin America
